Selection

Each team consists of 10 players (no substitutes) with the following restrictions:

  • 1 Goalkeeper (GK)
  • 3 Defenders (DF)
  • 3 Midfielders (MF)
  • 3 Attackers (FW)

You may select any combination of players fulfilling the criteria above, without exceeding a total team value of Â£100m. Teams must be emailed to hillcroftfantasylacrosse@gmail.com by midnight 28th Sep 2016. Any teams submitted after this deadline will not be entered into the league.

Player Values

Values have been determined based on data from last season.

Transfers

A manager can transfer one player per week. Teams must comply with the selection restrictions at all times. Transfers must be emailed to hillcroftfantasylacrosse@gmail.com before midnight Friday for each gameweek, and are immediately binding.
